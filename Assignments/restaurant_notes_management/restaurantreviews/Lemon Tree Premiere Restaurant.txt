Lemon Tree Premiere Restaurant

Lemon Tree offers a cheerful ambience with bright interiors.

Food quality is good, especially their continental dishes.

The breakfast buffet is one of the highlights.

Service is friendly and welcoming.

Prices are moderate, offering value for money.

Vegetarian options are plenty and flavorful.

Sometimes the spice levels could be more consistent.

Seating is comfortable for long meals.

Hygiene and cleanliness are well maintained.

Overall, a pleasant dining experience for families and business travelers.
