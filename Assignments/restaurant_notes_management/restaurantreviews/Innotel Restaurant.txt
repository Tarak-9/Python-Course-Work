Innotel Restaurant

Innotel offers a wide variety of dishes with good presentation.

The interiors are modern and give a cozy feel.

Food quality is generally very good, especially Indian curries.

Service is professional, though sometimes a bit slow.

Prices are slightly on the higher side.

The dessert section is excellent, especially their chocolate dishes.

Good spot for corporate dinners and small gatherings.

The staff is helpful with menu recommendations.

Drinks and mocktails are refreshing and well-prepared.

Overall, a reliable fine-dining experience.
