Novotel Restaurant

Novotel’s restaurant has a luxurious ambience with stylish décor.

The buffet spread is excellent with wide international options.

The live counters make the dining experience interactive.

Service is very professional and courteous.

The desserts, especially cheesecakes, are outstanding.

Food quality is consistently fresh and well-cooked.

Prices are high but justified for a 5-star experience.

Sometimes waiting time is long during weekends.

Perfect for special occasions and celebrations.

Overall, an elegant dining spot with premium service.
