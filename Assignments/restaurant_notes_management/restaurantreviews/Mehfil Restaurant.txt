Mehfil Restaurant

The biryani at Mehfil is absolutely delicious and flavorful.

The spices are well balanced, giving an authentic Hyderabadi touch.

Service is quick even during peak hours.

Ambience is simple but clean and comfortable.

Prices are reasonable compared to the portion sizes.

Sometimes the seating area feels a little crowded.

The kebabs are juicy and worth trying.

Staff is polite and attentive.

Takeaway service is efficient and packaging is neat.

Overall, a great place for family dining with authentic taste.
