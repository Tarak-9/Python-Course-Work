Alankar Restaurant

Alankar is known for its traditional South Indian meals.

The thali is generous and served with authentic flavors.

Service is quick and staff is courteous.

Prices are pocket-friendly and suitable for daily dining.

Ambience is simple but clean and decent.

The dosas and idlis are soft and tasty.

Sometimes the restaurant gets too crowded during lunch hours.

North Indian dishes are decent but not their specialty.

Takeaway services are reliable.

Overall, a great spot for authentic South Indian cuisine lovers.
